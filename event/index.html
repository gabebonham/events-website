About Event

Crafty Cinema Night

​Join us for Crafty Cinema Night, where you can bring your favorite craft project and enjoy a classic film with your fellow Atlanta Craft Club Members <3

What to expect?

Meet us at The Springs Cinema & Taproom, settle in with your very own craft, enjoy a movie of Craft Club's choice The Devil Wears Prada. The only thing that's better than crafting cozily in your own space, is crafting cozily in a room full of people enjoying the same thing, and watching our favorite Anne Hathaway movie.

​Cancellation Policy

​​Unfortunately for this event tickets are non-refundable/exchange-able! Please double check your calendar. In the event you are no longer able to make it, you are welcome to give your ticket to a friend or let me know by emailing hello@atlantacraftclub.com and I'll give you a credit towards your next Craft Club event.​​
